The Bio

The blueprint, before it became a club.

With a career built on the fusion of elite athletic discipline and high-level nutritional science, Maria has spent the last seven years refining a methodology that respects the complexity of modern life. She recognized that for the high-performer, traditional wellness felt like a second job — one filled with guesswork and rigid restrictions. In 2019, she founded MAGOCLUB to solve that problem.

Operating in the heart of London's wellness scene, her methodology is rooted in the architecture of longevity. She does not believe in the fleeting intensity of the industry, but in building a sustainable, high-fidelity lifestyle that thrives long after the session ends. Today, Maria serves as the lead architect for her clients' lifestyles, ensuring their physical presence is as powerful as their professional one.
